Our story

Our company was created in the 2015 year and since that time, we have been teaching people to work with clay. We work out new techniques and made a lot of people happy.

In 2017 our company became bigger, we had hired 3 new teachers. We also bought new equipment and added one more class. The number of students increased by 10%. We started learning our students with new techniques and organized evening classes.
